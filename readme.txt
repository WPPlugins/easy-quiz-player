== Description ==

Easy Quiz Player is the most professional and versatile tool for creating a quiz on your WordPress page. Easy Quiz Player lets you publish your own quiz, with your own questions, on your page within minutes.

Live demo at <http://www.easyquizplayer.com>

1. Create your questions in Quiz Manager in a quick and easy way.
2. Choose from five different question types:
	- Text Questions
	- Image Questions
	- Scale Questions
	- Sort Questions
	- Video Questions
3. Choose a theme color that matches your brand.
4. Publish on your page with a simple shortcode
5. Get lots of leads and statistics from your users with our advanced analytics page.

= Features: =
 * 5 different question types
 * Video questions (YouTube, Vimeo)
 * Quiz Manager for easy quiz creating
 * Social media challenges (Facebook & Twitter)
 * Random question order
 * Different templates for custom design
 * Lead generator for collection of e-mail addresses
 * Statistics & analytics
 * Language support
 * Leaderboard
 * FREE and PRO subscriptions. PRO is free from ads and with all features enabled.

== Installation ==

1. Install the plugin on your WordPress page. Or upload it to your FTP in the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Now you should have a new menu in the admin called 'Easy Quiz Player'. Go there.
4. Create your free account and start creating your first quiz. Enjoy!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I embed the quiz? =

On each quiz page there is a shortcode to embed on your WP-site, just paste it into the editor and weâ€™ll do the magic :)
Just grab the shortcode on the same page as you creating your quiz (Quiz Manager) by clicking on the button 'Embed'.

= Can you add videos from Youtube and Vimeo? =

Yes, just get the embed code from the video and paste it into the video section when you edit a question.

= Does it work on Facebook? =

Yes. We recommend to create a Canvas App when embedding on Facebook. You can also link from Facebook to your website.

= Can I see who have played my quiz and see other statistics? =

Yeah, there is a full section for statistics and analytics. You can even download the top list of your registered players to an Excel file to use in your furthered marketing.
